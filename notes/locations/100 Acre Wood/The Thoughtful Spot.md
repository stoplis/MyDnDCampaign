# The Thoughtful Spot

> [!info] At a Glance
> A log at the edge of the Wood beside a slow stream, worn smooth from use. The grass around it is soft. The wind moves in the high trees. It is not much to look at. It is the place the Wood begins and ends.

## Description

There is nothing remarkable here except the quality of the quiet. The log is old enough to have sunk slightly into the earth on one side, making it level to sit on if you sit in the right place. The stream moves past without hurry. On a clear day, through the trees to the east, you can see the light changing at the Wood's boundary.

This is where expeditions begin. The North Pole Expedition started here. Many plans were made here and several of them went wrong in interesting ways and the Thoughtful Spot bore no ill will about it.

## Mechanical Note

A Short Rest taken at the Thoughtful Spot restores one additional Hit Die. Some places are good for the spirit. This is one of them.

## As Base Camp Anchor

The Thoughtful Spot is where the party should begin and end each visit to the Wood. It is the chapter-break location: where Rabbit waits when the party returns, where the debrief happens before the next chapter begins, where the party sits when they need to be quiet for a moment.

When all the Pooh characters are home, the Thoughtful Spot is never empty. Someone is always here, or just passing through. Pooh comes here to think. Piglet comes here to be with Pooh. Roo comes here to examine things. Eeyore comes here and stares at the stream for a long time and says something that is technically about the stream but is actually about everything.
