# Kanga and Roo's House

> [!info] At a Glance
> A neat, well-maintained house in the southeast of the Wood, near the Sandy Pit. It smells of something clean and warm. There is always a spare plate set at the table. Roo's muddy paw-prints track from the Sandy Pit to the front door as a matter of routine.

## Description

The house is tidy in the way of somewhere that is actually lived in rather than merely organised. The spare plate at the table is not a gesture; it has always been there, for whoever might come. The kitchen smells of something being prepared or recently finished. Kanga's medical cabinet is on the wall near the back — locked (she has the key) and very well stocked.

Outside, the Sandy Pit is where Roo plays. It is a shallow bowl of pale sand among the roots of an oak. It has the quality of a place that is used every day and knows it.

## When Both Are Gone

Roo's muddy paw-prints still track from the Sandy Pit to the door, from the last time he came home. The spare plate is set. The kitchen is cold.

Under the sand of the Sandy Pit (DC 14 Investigation) is a forgotten toy of Roo's — a small carved donkey. In this campaign, it carries a strange weight.

Kanga's medical cabinet (DC 10 with her permission, DC 17 without it) contains supplies equivalent to a Healer's Kit and one Potion of Healing.

## When Roo is Home (Chapter 2 onward)

Fresh paw-prints in the Sandy Pit. The sand is recently disturbed. Roo is probably here or very recently wasn't.

## When Kanga is Home

The house is warm. Something is being made in the kitchen. The spare plate has been cleared and reset. Kanga comes to the door when the party approaches, because she heard them coming from quite far away and has had time to assess whether they need anything.

She will offer to look at any injuries. She should be taken up on this.
